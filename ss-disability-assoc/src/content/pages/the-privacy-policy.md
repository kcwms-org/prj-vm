## SS DISABILITY ASSOCIATES LLC. Privacy Policy

SS DISABILITY ASSOCIATES LLC. and its affiliates value the privacy of individuals who use our websites, app, and related
services (collectively, our "Services"). This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, and share the information of
users of our Services. By using our Services, you agree to the collection, use, disclosure, and processing of your
information as described by this Privacy Notice.

### Information We Collect

We may collect a variety of information about you or your devices from various sources, including:

- **Registration information** — name, email, phone number, or mailing address when you sign up or contact us.
- **Communications** — contents of messages or attachments you send to us.
- **Event registration** — name, address, title, company, phone number, or email for events you register for.
- **Payment information** — collected by our third-party payment processor.
- **Device & usage data** — IP address, browser type, OS version, session lengths, pages viewed.
- **Cookies** — session and persistent cookies to help us understand how you use our Services.

### Informed Consent to be Contacted by Telephone

By registering on the Site, you agree that such act constitutes an inquiry and/or application for purposes of the Amended
Telemarketing Sales Rule and applicable state do-not-call regulations. You consent to receive telephone calls, including
artificial voice and pre-recorded messages, to the telephone number(s) you provided.

### How We Use Your Information

- To provide, maintain, improve, and enhance our Services
- To communicate with you and respond to your requests
- For marketing and advertising purposes
- To detect and prevent fraud
- For legal compliance purposes

### How We Share Your Information

We may share information with service providers retained in connection with the provision of our Services. No mobile
information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. We use Google, Meta,
Facebook, LinkedIn, and Microsoft services for analytics and advertising.

### Your Choices

You may unsubscribe from promotional emails via the link in those emails. You can manage cookies through your browser
settings. To opt out of SMS, reply STOP to any message.
